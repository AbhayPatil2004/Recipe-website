Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Mikl
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Make Syrup: Boil sugar, water, and cardamom until syrup is sticky but not too thick. Add saffron if desired. Set aside warm
  2. Make Dough: Mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, and ghee. Add milk slowly to form a soft dough. Avoid over-kneading.
  3. Shape Balls: Divide dough into small, smooth balls with no cracks.
  4. Fry: Heat oil on low-medium. Fry balls until golden brown, stirring gently for even color.
  5. Soak: Drain fried balls and add to warm syrup. Let them soak for 1-2 hours.
  6. Serve: Enjoy warm or chilled!

Rasgullas

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar
  • Mikl
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Make Chhena: Boil milk and add lemon juice/vinegar to curdle. Strain through a muslin cloth, rinse with cold water to remove the acidic taste, and squeeze out excess water.
  2. Knead Dough: Knead chhena until smooth and soft (about 5-7 minutes). Form small, crack-free balls.
  3. Prepare Syrup: Boil sugar and water in a large pot. Add cardamom pods if desired.
  4. Cook Rasgullas: Drop balls into boiling syrup, cover, and cook on medium heat for 15-20 minutes until they double in size.
  5. Rest: Let the rasgullas cool in the syrup. Enjoy chilled or at room temperature!
